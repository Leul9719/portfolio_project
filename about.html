I'm currently working through the Nexus Frontend bootcamp and a DSA course in Python, learning to build things from the ground up the same way I learned circuits: slowly, and by actually understanding why something works, not just copying a solution.Right now, this portfolio you're looking at is one of my first real projects. It's not polished yet, and that's fine I'd rather build something real and rough than fake something perfect. My goal over the next while is to keep building, land an internship and eventually freelance in AI and automation. I'm not in a rush. I'd rather get good than get fast.
